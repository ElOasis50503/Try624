Costa Rica · Ley N° 7933 · 1999 · Amended 2022 (Ley 10229) & 2025 (Ley 10746)
Ley Reguladora de la Propiedad en Condominio
Reference

The national law governing all condominium property in Costa Rica. Defines private and common areas, owner rights and obligations, fee structures, assembly voting thresholds (unanimity / 66.67% / simple majority), and the administrator's mandate. The 2022 reform reduced the threshold for amending the reglamento from unanimity to two-thirds of the total condominium value — a significant change for HOAs seeking to update governance documents.
