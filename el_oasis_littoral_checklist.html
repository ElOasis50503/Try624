Dry Season · Dec–Apr

Upwelling active. Cooler sea-surface temperatures, higher salinity, increased productivity, and denser barnacle and limpet cover.

Wet Season · May–Nov

Upwelling reduced. Warmer sea-surface temperatures, algal bloom, expanded macroalgae, and greater gastropod mobility.

Source: Sibaja-Cordero, J.A. & Cortés, J. (2008). Temporal variation in rocky intertidal communities, NW Costa Rica. Revista de Biología Tropical, 56(Suppl. 4), 107–131. Bahía Salinas reference baseline.
