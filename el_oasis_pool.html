The pool and its central planted island, looking south toward the garden boundary.
I

On the Deck

The pool sits at the heart of the property's hardscape, but it doesn't sit apart from the garden — the central planted island and the surrounding palms keep it inside the same dry-forest character as the rest of the grounds. Blue tile, white balustrade, and the diamond-inlaid columns at each corner give the deck its own formal note, a counterpoint to the looser plantings elsewhere on the property.

Kept by Carlos and Gonzalo
